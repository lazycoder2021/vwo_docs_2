Signing Up for VWO Free Trial Account

Author
  • Updated
  • October 19, 2022 12:15
Share:
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn
Follow

With VWO free trial account, you can test out all the tools which VWO web analytics offers for free. Once the trial period ends, you will have to switch over to a paid account for continued usage. In this article, let's take a look at how you can sign up for a VWO free trial account.

Click 'Try VWO FOR FREE' button in the VWO page [or click one of the free trial CTAs that you come across the VWO website] and do the following.

  • Provide Your Personal Details
  • Verify Your VWO Account Via Email
  • Set Up Your Basic Account
  • Select Products/Tools That Suit Your Requirements
  • Set Up Goal To Track Your Website Conversions
  • Enable Insights To Analyze/Track Visitor Behaviour
  • Install VWO Smart Code
  • Or Mail Your Developer The Smart Code

NOTE:   No need to provide your credit card details for free trial account.

 

Provide Your Personal Details

When you enter your business email and click 'TRY VWO FOR FREE' button, the VWO sign up pop up would appear which will prompt you to enter your personal information to get started.

  1. Provide your First Name, Last Name, Phone Number and Password.
  2. Then click CREATE ACCOUNT button to proceed further.

Verify Your VWO Account Via Email

Once you sign up, it's highly recommended that you complete your account verification immediately. Only then you'll be able to use VWO products to the fullest during your trial period.

  1. Check your email for 'Your VWO Account is created' mail.
  2. Click on the verification link in the mail.
  3. Your account will be verified instantly.

Set Up Your Basic Account

This is where you provide the URL of your website which needs to be optimized for conversions. You should also specify the industry your business falls under along with your website's average monthly traffic (if you are aware of it), so that VWO can understand your current web presence and optimize it accordingly.

  1. Provide your enterprise website's URL in Which website do you want to optimize? box.
  2. Select your website's average monthly traffic from What's your average monthly traffic dropdown? dropdown (if you are not sure of it, select Not sure option).
  3. Select the industry your enterprise falls under from What's your industry? dropdown.
  4. Click Next button to proceed further.

NOTE: You can also make use of tooltip icons to guide you to the select the right options.

Select Products/Tools That Suit Your Requirements

It is recommended that you go with the default Conversion Optimization Essentials pack as this should be enough to get you started. This pack includes the following products: VWO Testing, VWO Insights, VWO Deploy and VWO Plans.

  • VWO Testing: helps you with running different tests like A/B Testing, Multivariate Testing, Split URL Testing etc., on your website. Visit VWO Testing to learn more.
  • VWO Insights: helps you track your website visitor journey and make informed decisions based the results. Visit VWO Insights to learn more.
  • VWO Deploy: helps you self-deploy the changes to your website without having to reach out to VWO support team. Visit VWO Deploy to learn more.

Click Next to proceed further.


Set Up Goal To Track Your Website Conversions

Goal helps you define what you want to achieve with your website. One obvious goal (and therefore the default option) is 'Generate More Leads'.

Select Goal Type

  1. Select a specific goal option that reflects what you want to achieve (or go with the default 'Generate More Leads' goal option. And once you get acquainted with the product, try out other goals later).

Specify Goal Details

You will also be required to provide the metric or standard against which the goal is going to be measured (the Measure leads by option) and the page you want to setup for tracking your goal.

  1. Measure leads by dropdown lets you measure leads (for the webpage being setup) by letting you choose from one of the two available options: i) tracking page visits (default option) and ii) tracking form submits.
  2. Specify the URL of the webpage you want to track in Which page do you want to track? box. Some examples of the webpages you might want to track first up: i) any landing page of your website, if you had selected 'tracking page visits' option or ii) contacts page or send us your queries page which contains form, if you had selected tracking form submits option. Because these are some common pages where most of your website conversions happen.
  3. Click Next to proceed further.

Enable Insights To Analyze/Track Visitor Behaviour

You can analyze your website visitor behavior by using the following: i) Heatmaps and Scrollmaps, ii) Session Recordings and iii) Form Analytics. All 3 options are enabled by default.

  • Heatmaps and Scrollmaps: These maps basically indicate visitor hotspots within your website, so that you can make informed decisions accordingly. Visit Heatmaps and Scrollmaps to learn more.
  • Session Recordings Records visitors sessions and replays them for you, so that you can understand what engages your website visitors and what doesn't. Visit Session Recordings to know more.
  • Form Analytics: Analyzes your website forms for usability. Lets you know which form fields website visitors are comfortable filling out and fields they have a hard time dealing with. Visit Form Analytics to know more.

Click Next to proceed further.


Install VWO Smart Code

VWO smart code is what helps you connect your website to VWO servers so that the necessary tests and analyses can be run on your website. It's basically a JavaScript code snippet which needs to be included between <head> </head> tags of your website's home page.

  1. Click clipboard icon to copy the smartcode being displayed.
  2. Paste it between the <head> </head> tags of your website's home page

Or Mail Your Developer The Smart Code

If you are not the one who is going to connect your website to VWO servers (which is most often the case), you can mail the smart code snippet to your developer team so that they can connect it for you. And don't worry, you don't have to give any explicit instructions to them, VWO's predefined mail would be sufficient enough to get the message across.

  1. Click Send to Developer button. Doing so will open up 'Email VWO SmartCode to Your Developer' pop up.
  2. Enter your development team's mail id in the To box.
  3. Modify the Subject if required (though, this is not necessary).
  4. Click Send button.
NOTE:   VWO Smart Code is of 2 types: Synchronous and Asychronous. The one that is recommended is Asynchronous Smart Code. It is the one which gets displayed by default.

 

Clicking Next button will display a 'Your set up is complete!' success message, indicating that now you are ready to use VWO products on your website for improving its conversion.
